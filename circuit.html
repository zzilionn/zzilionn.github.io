Inner Circuit

A video installation on the investigation of the self through a technological gaze, in an act of co-regulation between the individual and the machine. A circular process that embraces and dissects psychosexual facets of the self and emotional bodies. A disruptive process that both destroys and builds multitudes of inner worlds.
